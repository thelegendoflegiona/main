THE LEGEND OF LEGIONA

More than a nation —
a legacy.

The Legend of Legiona, known as The LoL, is a sovereign nation within the Skyxion world. Originally founded as a small squad called "The Sus" by Faiz4224, ItzDynozz, and Imii Kun, the nation rapidly grew into the most modern and wealthiest in all of Skyxion.

Through democratic elections, political conflict, a presidential crisis, and full recovery — The LoL has proven that unity and perseverance can overcome anything.

"In the Name of Unity, Legacy, and Glory." — Proclamation of Sovereignty

The Three
Founders.

The Legend of Legiona was built by three people who turned a simple squad into a sovereign nation. Their names are inscribed in the Proclamation of Sovereignty — the founding document of The LoL. Their legacy lives on in every block, every railway track, and every citizen of The Legend of Legiona.

01
F

Faiz4224

FIRST PRESIDENT // FOUNDING LEADER

Whose unwavering resolve and ingenuity laid the foundation of this great nation. Led The LoL through its golden era and rebuilt it stronger after the political crisis of 2023.

02
I

Imii Kun

CO-FOUNDER // VISIONARY

The visionary who proposed renaming the nation to "The Legend of Legiona." A pillar of strength whose dedication forged the bonds of unity that sustain Legiona to this day.
